APP DEVELOPMENT

The Registration Page is a fundamental component of this application, designed to facilitate user onboarding and account creation. Through a carefully constructed interface, users can input their essential information, such as name, email, password, and any additional required details. This page serves as the gateway for users to establish their presence within the platform, enabling access to exclusive features or personalized experiences. Ensuring a seamless and secure registration process was a priority in its design, aiming to enhance user convenience while maintaining data integrity. Crafting this Registration Page involved meticulous attention to user experience and security measures, leveraging intuitive design principles to encourage engagement and facilitate hassle-free account creation for our users.

The application is a comprehensive repository offering information on three prominent online banking apps: POSB, OCBC, and UOB. Each section within the app is dedicated to providing users with easy access to these banking platforms. Upon selecting a specific bank, users are seamlessly redirected to the respective bank's website, granting them direct access to their online banking services.
